HDDs

HDD or Hard Disk Drive is a mechanical drive to store all of your information. HDD has a magnetic disk inside it where it stores the data. It spins at a rapid speed which can be measured through RPM (Revolutions Per Minute) which is different per HDDs depending on the company. It can also be used as a boot drive, but it is not optimal since SSDs are faster for boot drives. HDDs are mostly used to store files and such.
