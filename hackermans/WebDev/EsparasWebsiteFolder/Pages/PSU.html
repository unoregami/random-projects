Power Supplies

Power Supply, also known as PSU (Power Supply Unit), is what powers your computer/regulates the correct amount of DC voltages that your computer need. You get different wattage ratings for power supplies. The higher the wattage, the higher the electrical current that can be made available to everything that needs it to function properly. Buy PSU accordingly with your system since higher Watts, the more it will cost.
